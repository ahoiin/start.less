Paragraphs

Start.less's global default font-size is 13px, with a line-height of 18px. This is applied to the <body> and all paragraphs. In addition, <p> (paragraphs) receive a bottom margin of half their line-height (10px by default).

You can apply the classes .left, .right, .center, .multiColumn, .abstract to all paragraphs.
